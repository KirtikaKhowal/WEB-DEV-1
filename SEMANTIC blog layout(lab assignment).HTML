India is a vast and ancient country, home to over 1.3 billion people.New Delhi serves as its bustling capital city. The nation boasts a rich and long-standing history that spans millennia. India is renowned for its profound contributions to the fields of mathematics and science.Philosophical traditions originating in India have greatly influenced global thought. The country is a mosaic of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions.Its vibrant culture includes a rich tapestry of art, music, and dance. India's geography is incredibly varied, featuring the Himalayas, plains, and coastal regions.
