EIFEL TOWER IS THE MOST FAMOUS LANDMARK OF PARIS,FRANCE The Eiffel Tower is a famous wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France, built by Gustave Eiffel's company for the 1889 World's Fair. Standing at 324 meters (1,063 feet), it was the world's tallest man-made structure until 1930 and remains a global symbol of Paris and France.

TAJ MAHAL IS LOCATED IN AGRA,INDIA ALSO IT A PART OF SEVEN WONDER IN THE WORLD The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan between 1632 and 1648 in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

INDIA GATE IS LOCATED IN NEW DELHI,CAPITAL OF INDIAIndia Gate is a prominent war memorial and monument located in New Delhi, designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens and built between 1921 and 1931. This 42-meter-tall structure, made of red and yellow sandstone, commemorates over 13,000 Indian soldiers who died in World War I and the Third Anglo-Afghan War, with their names inscribed on its walls.
